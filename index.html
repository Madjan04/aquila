THE GUILD

Aquila (which means "eagle" in English and is pronounced "ah-kwi-la"), is a constellation on the celestial equator. In GrandChase Classic, it is a guild that operates on discord where guild events and other game-related activities occur. Aquila is open for both fresh and veteran players. The Guild Master, Madjan, and his co-founders, Akanechii and Suzhiyo, created Aquila with the intention of setting up a platform for all kinds of players to interact and be around people with the same interests. In Aquila, each member (regardless of their role) are treated equally, meaning you can befriend anyone, even the guild's Guardians and Wardens.

The guild runs with a casual community of Chasers who play for fun and in their own time. But of course, there are rules that one must follow. Fret not, these rules are not suffocating, they were set to keep the community safe from toxicity and people with ill intent.

If you wish to be a part of this kind of community, soar with us and make new connections in Aquila! Scroll down to learn more.

SERVER ROLES

Guild Master

Owner of the guild. Member with the highest position.

Aquila Warden

Administrators of the guild. Can be acquired through promotion from a Guardian.

Aquila Guardian

Moderators of the guild. Can be acquired through promotion based on specific requirements.

Celestial

Server Booster. Can be acquired by boosting the server.

Supreme Aquilian

Honorable role. Can be acquired by earning 50,000 guild points.

Senior Aquilian

Honorable role. Can be acquired by earning 40,000 guild points.

Stellar Aquilian

Honorable role. Can be acquired by earning 30,000 guild points.

Aquila Paladin

Honorable role. Can be acquired by earning 50,000 guild points.

Guild Member

Verified member. Given to new aquilians once they earn 1,000 guild points.
